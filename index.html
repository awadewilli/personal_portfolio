Who I Am

A Native South Carolinian, I am a junior web developer with graphic design sensabilities. I am a graduate of the Clemson University Graphic Communications program and have been working in the Printing Industry for the past four years.During that time, my passion for cool technology,problem solving, and creative power led me to seek new opportunities in the world of web developement by joining the Front-End Engineering course at The Iron Yard Academy. Through a rigorous three months, my time there taught me much about the world of the internet and about myself. I am happy to say I now have the tools to solve real problems and build awesome things in nearly any medium.

The Karaoke Man

The Karaoke Man is an event management system that allows users to easily host and join karaoke parties. As the final project from my time at the Iron Yard I was tasked with creating a multipage application with CRUD capabilities that solved a unique problem. To do this I partnered with a back-end developer to design our database while, I designed the dynamic user interface and graphics. This application is built with a Django/Python back-end, Backbone JS, and React JS.
